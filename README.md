## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is CollabSuite and who is it for?
CollabSuite is an all-in-one productivity and collaboration platform designed for remote, hybrid, and distributed teams. It combines project management, team chat, file sharing, time tracking, and calendar integration into a single, intuitive workspace. Whether you’re a startup, agency, or product team, CollabSuite helps you stay organized and aligned.

******

2. Do I need any technical experience to set it up?
Not at all! CollabSuite is built to be user-friendly from the start. You can create a workspace, invite your team, and start managing projects in just a few clicks — no coding or IT support required.

******

3. Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with access to all core features — no credit card required. Try it out with your team and see how it fits your workflow.

******

4. How many users can I invite to my workspace?
As many as you’d like. Our platform scales with your team — whether you’re a solo freelancer or a 500+ person organization. Plans are priced per user, so you only pay for what you need.

*******

5. Is my data safe with CollabSuite?
Absolutely. We take security seriously. CollabSuite uses end-to-end encryption, secure cloud hosting, role-based access control, and complies with GDPR. Your files, conversations, and data are protected at every level.

******

6. What tools does CollabSuite integrate with?
We integrate with a wide range of tools including: Slack, Google Drive, Outlook, Zoom, GitHub, Notion, Dropbox, Trello, and more. New integrations are added regularly based on user feedback.

*******

7. What support options do you offer?
You can reach our team 24/7 via email or live chat. We also have an extensive Help Center with guides, tutorials, and troubleshooting steps. Dedicated support is available for teams on our Premium and Enterprise plans.

******

8. Can I use CollabSuite with clients or external collaborators?
Yes! You can invite clients, freelancers, and external partners to specific workspaces or projects. With role-based permissions, you control exactly what they can see and do.

******

9. Can I upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime?
Yes. Our plans are flexible — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. No hidden fees or lock-ins.

******

10. Where can I learn more about how to use CollabSuite?
Our Help Center is full of articles, video tutorials, and best practice guides. You can also check out our Blog for expert tips on team productivity, remote work, and more.
